COMMON QUESTIONS

Quick Answers

How long does a custom commission take?

Typical turnaround is 3-4 weeks from design approval to delivery. Rush options available for an additional fee.

Do you accept international orders?

Yes! We ship worldwide with tracking and insurance. Shipping costs calculated at checkout.

Can I visit the studio without an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome during open hours, but we recommend booking an appointment for design consultations to ensure artist availability.

What's your return policy for customs?

Each pair is made to order. Returns are not accepted, but we offer touch-up services and quality guarantees for craftsmanship issues.
